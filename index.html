What Nudge does

  • Verified Amazon purchases

    Nudge automatically confirms you're buying from a trusted seller on Amazon and ensures your purchase link is clean and affiliate-tagged for your protection.

  • Facebook Marketplace scam alerts

    Real-time warnings on Facebook Marketplace listings that show signs of fraud — suspicious pricing, duplicate photos, or unverified seller patterns.

  • Rental listing trust scores

    Nudge scans Craigslist and rental listings for red flags like too-good-to-be-true pricing, copied descriptions, or mismatched contact details — before you enquire.
